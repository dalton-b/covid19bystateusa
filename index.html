COVID-19 Reported Active Cases by US State

These graphs show the number of active cases of COVID-19 in US States and territories over time. I'm operating under the assumption that each person is no longer contagious 28 days after they've been tested. Keep in mind that these are only reported cases, and the actual number of active cases may be much higher.
